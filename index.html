ONS Postcode Directory look-up

Enter a postcode to get information from the latest ONS Postcode Directory.

Help with downloading a CSV
  1. Zoom in or search the map to find a postcode
  2. Click the round blue marker (called a postcode centroid)
  3. Click the ellipsis (…) icon in the bottom right of the pop-up
  4. Click the "View in Attribute Table" link
  5. Click "Options" in the Attribute Table
  6. Click "Export all to CSV"
